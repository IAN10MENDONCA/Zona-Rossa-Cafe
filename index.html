About Zona Rossa

Located in the main reception hall of The Castle Centre, Zona Rossa Cafe is designed for everyday convenience, whether you are grabbing a coffee, refuelling after a workout, or sitting down for a fresh meal.

Interior-Led Identity

The visual direction reflects the real space: deep burgundy walls, black chalkboard-style menus, warm timber accents, and a simple, community-focused setup.

What Sets It Apart

Affordable pricing, generous portions, smoothies alongside café classics, and a friendly local energy that feels approachable rather than overdesigned.
